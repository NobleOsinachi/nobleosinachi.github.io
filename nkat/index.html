Output: Clear strategy doc + roadmap for your funnel

Creative & Copy Blueprint

I create a complete blueprint for your funnel. This includes ad creative concepts (video specs, messaging angles), landing page structure, copy frameworks, and email sequences. You'll see the entire vision before we build.

Funnel Build & Tech Setup

I build your complete funnel: edit your ad creatives, write final copy, build landing pages, set up email sequences, integrate your CRM, and configure automations. Everything connects. Everything works.

Output: Profitable campaign + optimization report

Scale & Ongoing Support

Once we find what works, we scale it. I can manage ongoing ad spend, create new creatives, test new audiences, and continuously optimize. Your funnel becomes a reliable revenue machine.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before we work together

Who is this for?

This is for founders and business owners who are serious about growth. You might be a coach with a digital offer, an e-commerce brand, a SaaS founder, or someone running a lead generation business. What you all have in common: you know your offer is good, but you need a system to turn that offer into consistent revenue.

Do you only work with certain niches?

No. I've built funnels in fitness, finance, e-commerce, SaaS, info products, coaching, and more. The principles of direct response and funnel architecture work everywhere. What matters is that your offer solves a real problem and you're willing to invest in the system to sell it.

What do you need from me to get started?

Not much. You need:

  • A clear offer (product, course, service, coaching program)
  • Understanding of your target audience
  • Willingness to invest in traffic (ads)
  • Access to your tools (email platform, website, ad accounts)

I'll handle the strategy, creative execution, and optimization. You just need to show up for calls and make decisions.

How long does a funnel project typically take?

  • Strategy & planning: 1-2 weeks.
  • Creative & copy production: 2-3 weeks.
  • Funnel build & launch: 1-2 weeks.
  • First optimization cycle: 2-4 weeks.

    • So typically 6-12 weeks from kickoff to a performing funnel.

That said, you'll see results progressively—not all at the end.
By week 4, you'll have traffic.
By week 8, you'll have data on conversions.
By week 12, we're optimizing for profitability.

(Timelines may be shorter depending on scope, readiness, and speed of feedback.)

Do you manage ongoing ads, or just setup?

Both. During the project, I manage ads as part of the Launch & Optimize phase. After that, it depends on your package:

  • Starter: I manage up to $500/day budget
  • Growth: I manage up to $3k/day budget
  • Scale: Unlimited, plus multi-channel management

You're never left hanging. I'm continuously testing, optimizing, and scaling what works.

What's your payment structure?

Monthly retainer, paid upfront. This aligns incentives—you're my client for the duration, and my job is to make sure every month delivers results.

We do minimum engagements (3 months for Starter, 2 months for Growth, custom for Scale). This gives us time to build and test properly.

Can you work with my existing tools and platforms?

Yes. I work with any platform: Shopify, ConvertKit, ClickFunnels, Leadpages, custom WordPress, you name it. I integrate with Zapier, Make, webhooks, APIs—whatever you need to connect your ecosystem.

If you don't have tools, I'll recommend the best stack for your situation.

What if the funnel doesn't perform?

We test and iterate. If your initial angle doesn't work, we test new creative, new copy, new landing pages, new audiences. That's what I do. We'll keep testing until we find what works.

What I won't do is blame you or your offer. My job is to find the right message and channel. If we've tested thoroughly and nothing's working after 8-10 weeks, we'll be honest about it—but this rarely happens with the right offer and targeting.
