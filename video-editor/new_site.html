Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? I've got answers.

What's your turnaround time?

For standard projects, I deliver within 3-5 business days depending on the package and complexity. For urgent requests, I offer expedited services at an additional fee. Just let me know your timeline, and I'll do my best to accommodate.

Do you provide revisions?

Yes! All packages include 1-2 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revision rounds can be arranged at a small additional fee.

What type of files do you need from me?

I can work with most video and audio formats. For content repurposing, simply provide your long-form content (podcast, webinar, etc.), and I'll handle the rest. For custom projects, I'll provide a detailed list of required assets after our initial consultation.

Do you offer script writing services?

Absolutely! With my background in direct response copywriting, I can craft compelling scripts for your videos that drive engagement and conversions. This service can be added to any package or purchased separately.

Which social platforms do you optimize for?

I create optimized content for all major social platforms including TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Facebook, and LinkedIn. Each platform has different requirements and best practices, which I carefully consider during the editing process.
